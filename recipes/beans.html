How to make Porridge Beans

Ingredients

  • Raw beans
  • Red oil
  • Diced onions
  • Grinded fresh pepper
  • Grinded garlic and ginger
  • Grinded crayfish
  • meat stock
  • Spices eg: maggi, salt

Steps

  1. Pick the raw beans (Separate the beans from the debris)
  2. Wash the picked beans with water and perboil till it's soft
  3. Seive the perboiled beans and leave in seive
  4. Pour some red oil in a pot on fire and when it is a bit hot, add the diced onions and stir to bleach a little
  5. Add the reamining ingredients and spices to the sauce
  6. Now add the perboiled beans in the sauce and add water to cover the beans
  7. Taste the porridge and add required spices if necessary
  8. Leave to cook
  9. Your porridge beans is ready, dish and enjoy
