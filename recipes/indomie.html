Indomie

Description

How to make indomie

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Diced carrots
  • 1/2 Cup Chopped Green bean
  • Diced Onioins and pepper
  • 2 Packs of Noodles
  • Noodle Follow-come spices

Steps

  1. Set a pot on heat, add two cups of water and allow to boil
  2. Boil eggs separately, deshell and set aside
  3. In the pot with boiling water, combine noodles, pepper, onions, carrots, and green beans
  4. Allow cooking for 3 minutes, add the follow come spices, allow another minute
  5. Stir together and serve with the boiled eggs
